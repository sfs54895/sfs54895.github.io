A trip down memory lane

When I was younger, I really loved playing soccer! I was on a team at my local JCC where we would play every weekend, and I used to carpool with a lot of my really good friends from childhood to each game. Even though I wasn't really that good at soccer (never really scored a goal), it is still one of my favorite memories of my childhood.

In the first grade, I was in a play all about the different types of cheese. Honestly, I don't know why my school put on this play, but it was pretty funny looking back at it. Velveeta is my favorite cheese, hands down.

This is me dressed up as a witch for Halloween in 2nd grade! Halloween Town 2 was my favorite Disney movie, so I dressed up as a witch. That movie was the G.O.A.T.. If you wanna see some cool witches (like I was when I was younger), click here.
